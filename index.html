A lifetime of change

Leaving the future generation hope for a better planet

By Julia Janicki, Daisy Chung. Additional work by Jacki Whisenant

Globally, wildfires, heatwaves, extreme weather events have become more frequent in recent years as we experience more and more record-breaking temperatures. These are symptoms of change that has been happening in the background for some time now. How does our current world compared to that when we were born? With this context of how our planet has changed throuout the course of our lives, let's think about how our collective decisions can shape a future for the youth and future human beings. Shared Socioeconomic Pathways (SSPs) are climate change scenarios projecting societal, demographic and economic changes up to 2100.
